My Roots

I was born in the German city of Dresden, a week after East Germany closed its borders to the West and started to build the Berlin Wall in the middle of the Cold War.
The place I grew up in and went to school is Görlitz, the easternmost city in Germany. It is located on the border with Poland and boasts one of the most picturesque historic old town districts in Germany.
