My Career Stages

  1. 1982-1987: Studies of Physics at the Leipzig University, Germany (Diplom Physiker)
  2. 1987-1991: Institute for Semiconductor Physics in Frankfurt (Oder), Germany
  3. 1991-1996: Mitsubishi Electric Corp. in Itami and PhD in Electrical Engineering from Osaka University, Japan
  4. 1996-1999: Joined Siemens for a project with IBM and Toshiba in East Fishkill, New York, USA
  5. 1999-2001: From Siemens Semiconductors to Infineon Technologies AG in Munich, Germany
  6. 2001-2003: Joint project of Infineon and Toshiba in Yokohama, Japan
  7. 2003-2016: Various projects at Infineon Technologies Austria in Villach, Austria
  8. 2016-2018: On-site for joint project with Panasonic in Osaka, Japan
    2018-2022: Renewable energy projects for BayWa r.e. and independent consultant in Tokyo, Japan
    2022-present: Infineon Technologies Japan in Tokyo, Japan
